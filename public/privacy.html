Privacy Policy · Last updated March 2026

Slothr ("we", "our", "us") is a JEE Advanced study tracker. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

When you sign up, we collect your email address and (if you use Google Sign-In) your name and profile picture. We store your study sessions, goals, and practice test results so your progress persists across devices.

How we use it

Your data is used solely to power Slothr's features — tracking your study time, showing your analytics, and generating AI-powered study suggestions. We do not sell your data to any third party.

AI features

Slothr uses Anthropic's Claude API to generate personalised study suggestions. Anonymised summaries of your study patterns (not your personal details) are sent to the API to generate these suggestions.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL). Study data is tied to your account and is deleted when you delete your account.

Third-party services

We use Google OAuth for authentication and Supabase for data storage. Their respective privacy policies apply.
